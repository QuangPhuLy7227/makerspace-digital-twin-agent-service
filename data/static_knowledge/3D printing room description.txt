The 3D printing room is laid out to be in the shape of a square, with the door to the room being on the right side when walking in. The wall of the door is a large window, and upon walking in you can see on the wall to the right of the person entering a large display of various 3D printers. On the left when walking in you can see 2 counters, one that holds finished projects, houses a sink for potential cleaning, followed by another counter that houses 5 3D printers on the side closest to the window. On the other side of that counter, are computers that students, workers, and the manager of the makerspace can access to edit 3D prints in various software's' such as Autodesk. Walking further towards the back of the room, across from the large wall of 3D printers are tables that students can assemble printed parts, smooth, and finish projects they are working on.
