- The Gannon Maker-Space is located on the sixth floor in iHack building.
- The maker-space has 3D printers, CNC machines, and laser cutters.
- Always follow safety protocols before using any equipment.
- Students must book time on the machines using the online portal.
- A supervisor must be present for first-time users.
- The space is open from 8 AM to 8 PM on weekdays.
